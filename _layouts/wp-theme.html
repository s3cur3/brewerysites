Features

Customizable Design

We make it easy to get a site that suits your branding.

Tweak the theme to use your brewery's logo, colors, fonts, & photos.

Ecommerce Support

Selling your merchandise online is easy to set up and easy to manage, thanks to The Modern Brewery's integration with WooCommerce, one of the Web's most popular (and powerful!) ecommerce platforms.

Responsive, Mobile-Ready Design

If you want your Web site to build your fan base, your site has to play nice with phones & tablets.

That's why The Modern Brewery adapts to whatever screen size you’re using—desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone.

Social Media Integration

The Modern Brewery makes it easy to add links to your company’s social media accounts, both in the page header and the sidebar. It includes great-looking icons for Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Google+.

Show Off Your Beers

With a custom layout for beer pages, plus easy ways to embed your beers in any page (or the site-wide sidebar), your brewery's repertoire has never looked better.

Support for Google Maps

Add a map of your location (complete with support for getting directions) on a page, or put one in your footer and have it appear on all pages.

Optimized for Search Engines

The theme is designed using SEO best practices, so that you get the most traffic possible out of your Web presence.

We use Schema.org markup (a.k.a. microformats or structured data) to enhance your site’s results in the search engines, and we support the Yoast SEO plugin for enhanced SEO.

Comprehensive Documentation

The bundled sample WordPress data makes it easy to get up and running quickly.

If you get stuck, the documentation can walk you through every step of recreating the demo site.
